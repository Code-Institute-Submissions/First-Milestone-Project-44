About Us

Providing excellent service to our clients is the key to the success of our company. Our team believes that the client should always be informed of their project status and our job is to guide and help you through the design process, based on our experience and qualifications. Obtaining planning permission can be daunting for most house owners especially for those who are doing this for the first time. There are many stages to take care of, in between the initial idea and the actual building work. That is why through our experience, we aim to provide a comprehensive service that makes the planning process simpler for the client. We believe in transparency of communication between ourselves and the client, in honest work and keeping it simple. With us you will never have to deal with hidden charges, as we always provide a written quotation listing out service and terms prior to start of all our projects. This gives the client a peace of mind as everything is on paper.
