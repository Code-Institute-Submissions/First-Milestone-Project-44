Schematic Design

This stage involes our presence on site to take the appropiate survey and produce the exsiting drawings, take photographs which are the base for our first ideas. Having an image of the exsiting situation and the brief from the client, we move into sketch ideas and concepts of the proposed project. We start to think about materials, lights and shadows, and the functionality of the spaces. We will present the concept of the project to the client, often with a storyboard of ideas and 3D models.
